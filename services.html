Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

How long does the recruitment process take?
The Candidate Pipeline package delivers results in 7-10 business days, while the Talent Magnet service provides your optimized job description within 3 business days after the discovery call.
What if I'm not satisfied with the candidates?
For the Candidate Pipeline service, if any candidate withdraws interest or becomes unresponsive before your initial contact (within 3 business days of report delivery), we provide a replacement profile at no additional cost.
Do you guarantee a successful hire?
We guarantee delivery of qualified candidates and professionally crafted job descriptions based on industry best practices. However, hiring decisions depend on your internal processes, interviews, and offer competitiveness.
Can I customize a service package?
Absolutely! We understand that every organization has unique needs. Contact us to discuss a customized solution that fits your specific recruitment challenges and budget.
